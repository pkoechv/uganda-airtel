Airtel Money Payment Process

1. Dial *185# on your phone.
2. Select "Send money".
3. Select "International transfer".
4. Select "Kenya".
5. Select "Airtel money".
6. Enter the recipient's phone number: 254758086505.

After making the payment, please click the "Done" button to confirm your payment.

If you need more information or assistance on how to make a payment, click the "Get Help" button.
